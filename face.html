Super America License Application



When you are ready, take the picture and then upload it. Once that is done select detect to get your results.

After this has been done you will get your MCS score. MCS stands for Model Citizen Score and based on this number you will be given certain privelleges if you are accepted for the Super America License.

You're MCS score is calculated and displayed above. As you move through the application your score will change based on your responses to the questions and you're overall behavior.

You will be able to request a change to any information that may be wrong. Gender identification status may not be changed.
